Disclaimer

The Transfer Counselor Website (TCW) is a one-stop repository of resources and tools for California's community college counselors. This website is NOT designed to be used by students or parents. The TCW does not take the place of a counselor on your campus. It is intended to assist California's Community College (CCC) counselors while working with CCC students to establish an appropriate path toward transferring from

a California community college to a 4-year university. If you are a student or parent on this site, we STRONGLY recommend that you set an appointment with your California community college counselor to develop a Student Education Plan. We are constantly working to improve it. Like the site? Got questions or suggestions? Let us know!
